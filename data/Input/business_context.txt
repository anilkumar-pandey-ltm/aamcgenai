# E-Commerce Platform Business Context

## Business Domain
Our e-commerce platform serves as a comprehensive online retail solution enabling merchants to sell products directly to consumers. The platform supports both B2C and B2B transactions across multiple regions globally.

## Business Objectives
- Increase customer conversion rate by 15% through an intuitive shopping experience
- Reduce cart abandonment by 20% with streamlined checkout processes
- Improve customer retention through personalized recommendations and loyalty programs
- Enable merchants to efficiently manage inventory and fulfill orders
- Ensure secure transaction processing and data protection
- Support multi-channel selling including web, mobile, and social media integrations

## Key Stakeholders
- End Customers: Consumers purchasing products
- Merchants: Businesses selling products on the platform
- Platform Administrators: Staff managing the overall platform
- Marketing Teams: Personnel responsible for promotions and campaigns
- Customer Support: Teams handling customer inquiries and issues
- Fulfillment Partners: Third-parties handling shipping and logistics

## Critical Business Processes
1. **User Management**
   - Account creation and authentication
   - Profile management and preferences
   - Loyalty program enrollment and tracking

2. **Product Catalog Management**
   - Product listing and categorization
   - Inventory management
   - Pricing and discount configuration

3. **Shopping Experience**
   - Product search and browsing
   - Filtering and sorting capabilities
   - Product recommendations
   - Recently viewed items tracking

4. **Cart and Checkout**
   - Add to cart functionality
   - Saved cart management
   - Multi-step checkout process
   - Payment processing
   - Order confirmation

5. **Order Management**
   - Order tracking
   - Order modification
   - Cancellation and returns processing
   - Invoicing and receipts

6. **Customer Support**
   - Help center and FAQs
   - Ticket management
   - Live chat support
   - Return merchandise authorization

7. **Analytics and Reporting**
   - Sales performance metrics
   - Customer behavior tracking
   - Inventory forecasting
   - Marketing campaign effectiveness

## System Architecture Components
- **Frontend Services**
  - Web storefront (React)
  - Mobile applications (iOS/Android)
  - Admin dashboard (Angular)

- **Backend Services**
  - User service (Java/Spring Boot)
  - Product catalog service (Java/Spring Boot)
  - Cart service (Node.js)
  - Payment processing service (Java/Spring Boot)
  - Order management service (Java/Spring Boot)
  - Recommendation engine (Python/Flask)
  - Search service (Elasticsearch)

- **Data Management**
  - Transactional database (PostgreSQL)
  - Product catalog database (MongoDB)
  - Analytics data warehouse (Snowflake)
  - Cache layer (Redis)

- **Integration Points**
  - Payment gateways (Stripe, PayPal)
  - Shipping providers (FedEx, UPS, DHL)
  - Tax calculation services
  - Email and notification services
  - Social media platforms

## Regulatory Compliance Requirements
- PCI DSS for payment processing
- GDPR for European customer data
- CCPA for California residents
- ADA compliance for accessibility
- Local tax regulations for multi-region operations

## Business KPIs
- Conversion rate: 3.2% (target: 4.5%)
- Average order value: $67 (target: $75)
- Customer acquisition cost: $22 (target: $18)
- Customer lifetime value: $240 (target: $280)
- Cart abandonment rate: 68% (target: 50%)
- Return rate: 12% (target: 8%)

## Recent Business Initiatives
- Loyalty program enhancement with tiered rewards
- Mobile app redesign for improved user experience
- Implementation of AI-powered product recommendations
- Expansion to new geographic markets
- Integration with social media shopping platforms
- Subscription-based purchasing options

## Known Business Risks
- Payment processing downtime impacts revenue directly
- Inventory synchronization issues lead to customer dissatisfaction
- Search functionality performance affects conversion rates
- Checkout process complexity increases cart abandonment
- Security breaches damage brand reputation and trust
- Performance issues during high-traffic sales events

## Business Continuity Priorities
1. Payment processing capabilities
2. Product catalog availability
3. Order placement functionality
4. Customer account access
5. Inventory accuracy
6. Fulfillment operations
