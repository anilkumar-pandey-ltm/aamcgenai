=== DESCRIPTION ===
As a discloser with a valid invitation link, in the scenario where the discloser has been archived before the discloser opens the invitation link, I want the customer messaging within the 'Invalid Link' pop-up displayed to be updated for improved experience.

=== ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA ===
**Given** I am a discloser with valid invitation link and subscriber has archived my discloser record
**When** I click on the invitation link or paste the invitation link URL in browser and open it
**Then** I want system to display the 'Invalid Link' pop-up with below revised customer messaging replacing all existing verbiage (except for pop-up header all messaging within pop-up will be replaced with below message):

> This link is no longer valid for **[Policy Name]**. Please contact **[List Contact Email]** or [Convey Support](https://www.aamc.org/contact-convey-support) at **(202) 909-2001**. Support hours are Monday - Friday 9 am - 5 pm ET; closed Wednesday between 3 pm - 5 pm ET.

Note: Convey Support (https://www.convey.org/contact-convey-support) - on click, the link should open in new browser tab

=== TECHNICAL DETAILS ===
### UI Changes Required:
- Update 'Invalid Link' pop-up modal messaging
- Preserve pop-up header (do not modify)
- Replace all existing body content with new message
- Include dynamic fields: [Policy Name] and [List Contact Email]
- Add hyperlink to Convey Support that opens in new tab
- Ensure proper formatting of phone number and support hours

### Business Context:
- Scenario: Archived discloser attempts to use invitation link
- Current Behavior: Generic invalid link message
- New Behavior: Specific, actionable message with contact information
- User Role: Discloser
- Trigger: Opening invitation link for archived discloser record
